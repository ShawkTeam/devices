⚠️ Your warranty will be void after installing a custom ROM.
⚠️ Everything on your device will be deleted. Don't forget to back up your device. 
-If your device is completely original and BL locked, follow the steps below: 
1. Enable developer options from settings. 
2. Open developer options and enable OEM lock status. 
3. Turn off your device. 
4. Press and hold the volume buttons (+ and -) on the device and connect your device to the computer. Your device will enter Odin mode. 
5. After the blue screen appears, press and hold volume + and BL will turn on. 
6. Boot your device back into Odin mode and then enter setup mode by pressing volume + key. 
7. Download the recovery software suitable for your device (twrp, orangefox, etc.). 
8. Install the USB drivers, open the Odin program, select the recovery software from the AP section in the program and the vbmeta from the BL section, then disable automatic reboot. 
9. Connect your device to the computer and start the installation. 
10. After it is installed, press and hold the device's power button and volume - button until the screen disappears, and then press and hold the power and volume + button until the recovery software appears. 

-ROM Installation: 
1. Download the ROM. 
2. Enter the recovery software and press the format data button and type YES to confirm. Then restart the recovery. [guide]
3. Then install the ROM on your device or via micro SD card. 
4. If you are using it, then you can install gapps. 
5. Enjoy.
